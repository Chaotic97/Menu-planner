Features

Everything you need to run
your kitchen smarter

No more juggling spreadsheets, paper lists, and mental math. One tool that handles the whole workflow.

Recipe Costing

Automatic food cost percentages. Track ingredient prices, see per-portion costs, and know your margins at a glance.

Allergen Tracking

Full EU 14 allergen detection. Auto-flags from ingredients, tracks per-guest allergies, and generates safe menus.

Menu Builder

Drag dishes into menus, organize by course, set covers, and see total food cost and allergen breakdown instantly.

Prep Lists

AI-generated task lists from your recipes. Knows what to prep first, what batches together, and what's last-minute.

Shopping Lists

Aggregated across all menus. Auto-converts units, flags what's in stock, and exports to purchase orders.

AI Assistant

Ask it anything — clean up a recipe, check allergens, plan your prep, scale for an event. It knows your data.

How It Works

From recipe to service
in three steps

PlateStack connects every part of your kitchen workflow so nothing falls through the cracks.

1

Build your dishes

Add recipes with ingredients, directions, and costs. Import from URLs, documents, or type them in. PlateStack handles unit conversion and allergen detection automatically.

2

Plan your menus

Drag dishes onto menus, set portion counts, and see your food cost and allergen breakdown update in real time. Scale for events with one click.

3

Prep and execute

Generate shopping lists and prep task lists. The AI assistant helps plan what to prep, in what order, and flags the details you'd otherwise forget.
